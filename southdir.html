Joji Sekhon Gill is a HR veteran, with over 24 years of experience. Her stints in organisations spread across North America, India, Europe and Asia, have given her extensive exposure to best practices in Global Human Resources. She has recently joined DuPont as Strategic Human Resources Director. Prior to this worked with Microsoft India for six years, as Human Resources Director-a role that made her responsible for managing doubling of the company's headcount from 3,000 to 6,000 people. Earlier, Joji has worked with Symantec, Veritas, and KLA-Tencor, USA. Joji has a Masters degree in Personnel Management and Industrial Relations from Panjab University, Panjab, India.
